About me

Creative professional based in NYC with experience in on set wardrobe styling, editorial, e-comm and beauty shootings, personal shopping/styling and closet edits.


How do I work
  • Creating a visual deck for the client and production team with different wardrobe ideas/options, mood boards
  • Putting together the outfits and confirming them with the client
  • Shopping/pulling from the showrooms, stores
  • Doing costume fittings with talent
  • Packing up all the confirmed looks, shoes and accessories to move in between locations
  • Keeping wardrobe department organized
  • Dressing and styling the actors
  • Working on set with the crew, making sure that all the outfits look great in the shot
  • Returning clothes to the stores, doing paperwork
